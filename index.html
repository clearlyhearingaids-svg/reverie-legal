Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, first name, and last name. If you sign in via Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information from those providers.

Voice Recordings: Your voice diary entries are transcribed entirely on your device using your device's built-in speech recognition. No audio data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third party. Only the text transcription is stored in your account.

Diary Entries: The text content of your diary entries is stored securely in our database, associated with your user account.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Reverie Voice Diary service — storing, displaying, and exporting your diary entries. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

3. Third-Party AI Services

Reverie does not use any third-party AI services to process your data. All speech-to-text transcription is performed on-device using your browser's Web Speech API or Apple's on-device speech recognition (on iOS). No voice data or diary content is sent to OpenAI, Google Cloud, or any other external AI provider.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Your diary entries and account data are stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. Data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data — including diary entries, profile information, and app passwords — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Reverie:

  • Authentication: Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In for account creation (optional)
  • Hosting & Database: Cloud infrastructure for secure data storage

These services only receive the minimum data necessary to provide their functions (e.g., email for authentication). They do not receive your diary content or voice recordings.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen within the app. Upon deletion, all your diary entries, profile data, and any stored credentials are permanently removed. You may also contact us to request data deletion.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations, you have the right to: access your personal data, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, export your data (available via the PDF export feature in Settings), and withdraw consent at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Reverie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, please contact us through the Contact Support form in the app's Settings, or email us at: reslipper@gmail.com
